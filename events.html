Sentec MPERC’22

Venue: Main Auditorium of NEDUET

Date: TBD

SENTEC, continuing its legacy, provided a platform for the exhibition of projects and showcase of extra ordinary engineering skills by organizing its annual Mega Project Exhibition and Robotics Competition.The main purpose of the event was to reveal the hidden talent of our youth to boost their scientific talents and provided its participants and audience with the opportunity to benefit from the inventive engineering based projects and innovative ideas that were displayed during the event.The competitions included Projects Exhibition, Robowar, National Aerial vehicle competition, Robohaste, Roborush, Circuit designing, Speed programming, Gaming, Quiz, Gesture Controlled Bot, Idea Presentation and Treasure Hunt.
