About Sentec.

The Society for Promotion of Science Engineering and Technology which also goes by the name SENTEC is one of the most esteemed societies of NED University of Engineering and technology. It has the privilege of being the only official society of the university which has been actively functioning for the past 20 years. The society aims to impart the students with exposure to the practical implementation of their courses. Throughout the years it has proved to not only provide a platform to students to showcase their talents but has encouraged the participants to hone their technical skills as well.

HISTORY OF SENTEC

Society of Science Engineering and Technology (SENTEC) has functioned as a platform providing body for the enhancement of innovative ideas and widespread education of vast technological applications for the last 22 years in NEDUET. Also privileged to be the only official science society of the most ancient engineering institution in South Asia, NED, SENTEC has a big responsibility on its shoulders for bridging up the gap between the young, capable and bright minds of our nation and the fast pace technological advancements all around the world. SENTEC, unlike other engineering societies active in various institutions of Pakistan, functions as a multidisciplinary platform engaging students from various backgrounds to use their learned knowledge and creative thoughts to come up with new, innovative and sustainable solutions.
